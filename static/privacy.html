Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Files you upload

Files uploaded to sh4r3d are stored temporarily on Amazon S3. They are automatically and permanently deleted after 24 hours. We do not open, examine, analyse, or share the content of any uploaded files. Files are stored solely to make them available to people with the share link you create.

User tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, third-party scripts, or any other mechanism to identify or profile visitors. No personal data is collected or sold.

Cookies

A session cookie is set only when you sign in with an invite token. It is used solely to keep you authenticated and contains no personal information. It is cleared when you sign out or when your browser session ends.

Logs

Standard AWS infrastructure logs (CloudFront access logs, Lambda logs) may be retained for operational and security purposes. These logs contain IP addresses and request metadata but are not used for user profiling.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue or reach out via the project repository.
